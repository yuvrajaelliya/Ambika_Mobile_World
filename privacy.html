Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Ambica Mobile World, we deeply prioritize your privacy. We may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and device details when you book a repair, reserve a new smartphone, or make a purchase directly at our Palanpur store.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for providing our dedicated repair services, processing your purchases effectively, and sharing important updates regarding your device's repair status. We do not sell, distribute, or rent your personal information to external third parties.

3. Data Security during Repairs

We strictly adhere to an Express Data-Safe Service protocol. Our certified technicians ensure that all personal data resting on your smartphone during intricate motherboard or screen repairs remains absolutely untouched, secure, and confidential. We prioritize keeping your memories safe.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, your data, or our practices, please contact us at contact@ambicamobile.in or visit our shop at Shop No 64, Old Market Yard, Palanpur.
